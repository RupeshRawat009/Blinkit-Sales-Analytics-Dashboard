# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **Blinkit sales data** to uncover key business insights related to **sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends**.

The dashboard helps stakeholders make **data-driven decisions** by providing a clear view of KPIs and sales distribution.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Blinkit operates in a highly competitive quick-commerce market where understanding **sales trends and customer preferences** is critical.

👉 The goal of this project is to:

* Track overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Analyze outlet performance
* Provide insights for business growth

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

* Outlet location type
* Outlet size
* Item type
* Fat content
* Sales data
* Ratings
* Number of items
* Outlet establishment year

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 📈 1. Sales Performance Analysis

* Total Sales: **$1.19M**
* Average Sales: **$141**
* Total Items Sold: **8406**

---

### 🏬 2. Outlet Analysis

* Compared sales across **Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3 cities**
* Analyzed outlet size (Small, Medium, High)
* Identified top-performing outlet types

---

### 🥗 3. Product Analysis

* Sales by item type (Fruits, Snacks, Dairy, etc.)
* Fat content analysis (Low Fat vs Regular)
* Category-wise performance

---

### ⭐ 4. Customer Experience

* Average Rating: **3.9**
* Insight into customer satisfaction

---

### 📅 5. Trend Analysis

* Sales trend by outlet establishment year
* Identified peak growth periods

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* 📍 **Tier 3 outlets generate highest sales**
* 🛒 **Supermarket Type 1 dominates revenue contribution**
* 🥗 **Regular fat products outperform low-fat items**
* 📈 Sales peaked around **2018 (highest outlet performance)**
* ⭐ Ratings are consistent (~3.9), indicating stable customer satisfaction

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Expand more outlets in **Tier 3 locations**
* Focus on **high-performing product categories**
* Improve low-performing outlet types
* Optimize product mix (based on fat content & category demand)
* Enhance customer experience to improve ratings
